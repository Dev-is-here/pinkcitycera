    About Us

    DR.DEEPAK ALAVADI Founder

    An ambitious and experienced entrepreneur, with a passion for latest technologies, continuous learning and developing new skills, Dr.Deepak has been a change catalyst in dental labs of India. He has been instrumental in bringing the CAD-CAM technology to Indian labs 15 years ago. Since then there has been no turning back. In his zeal to bring the best techniques from around the globe, he keeps reinventing and updating himself and the lab as well. Being a dentist himself, with a successful practice of 22+years, he easily understands the requisites of the dentists as well as their patients. Dr.Deepak is always available for the dentists to discuss about the best possible approach and other technical issues, for their cases.
